TRADOC Rules

Available Positions

  • -Deputy Commanding General [O9]

Requirements

  • – Individual must be at least E1+.
  • – Individual is willing to discharge from their current command/division if within any.
  • – Individual must not possess any recent dishonorable discharges from the Training and Doctrine Command; no dishonorable discharges within the past week.
  • Individuals must be disposed to complete their supervisions within the given period.
  • – Individual is willing to execute their work professionally and outstandingly.
  • – It is highly preferred if the individual applying has great communicative skills & decent activity.
  • – Individual must possess competency and maturity and is willing to use those characteristics within any situation whatever the circumstances.
  • – 13+
  • – Mature
  • – Responsible
  • – Smart
  • – Making good decisions
  • – Trustworthy
